# Organic Chemistry · Interactive

**Structure, reactivity, and synthesis of carbon compounds**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 3 · Undergraduate

## What You'll Learn

- Structure, bonding, and molecular orbitals
- Stereochemistry
- Substitution and elimination reactions
- Alkene and alkyne reactions
- Aromatic chemistry
- Carbonyl chemistry: aldehydes, ketones, carboxylic acids
- Amines, amino acids, and biomolecules

## References

- John McMurry, *Organic Chemistry* (9th ed., Cengage)
- Paula Y. Bruice, *Organic Chemistry* (8th ed., Pearson)
- David Klein, *Organic Chemistry* (4th ed., Wiley)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
